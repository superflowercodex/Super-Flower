About Super Flower

The Super Flower Cannabis Codex is a living cannabis classification system designed to catalogue, compare, and explore cannabis genetics through standardized specimen profiles.

Each specimen is assigned a unique Codex entry and evaluated across multiple attributes, creating a structured reference system for collectors, enthusiasts, patients, cultivators, and competitors.

Unlike traditional strain lists, the Codex focuses on measurable characteristics and specimen-based comparisons, allowing users to discover, document, and explore cannabis in a consistent and organized format.
